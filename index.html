ABOUT ME

Eftekhar Hossain received his Bachelorâ€™s Degree (2014-2018) in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ETE) from Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET), Bangladesh. After completed his graduation Eftekhar has served as a Lecturer (October 08-2018, July 07-2019) of department of EEE at Premier University, Chittagong, Bangladesh. Currently, he is working as Lecturer of department of ETE, CUET.

His research interest lies at the intersection of machine learning, natural language processing and computer vision. His recent research focuses on Bangla Language Processing (BLP) where his mission is to build AI technologies for various application of BLP. He has collaborated with Omar Sharif to work on several BLP projects including Sentiment Analysis, Language Modelling and Image Captioning under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Mohammed Moshiul Hoque.

Eftekhar has grown his passion in research since undergraduate time. At that time, he worked on various small projects of different areas including Embedded system design , Signal and image processing and web development. Besides, he has also interested in data science based projects. He is an active participant in online data science competitions such as Kaggle and Analytics Vidhya.
